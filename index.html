What Is Universal Bridge?

Universal Bridge is a cross-chain payment and token bridging solution that connects 20+ blockchains through a single interface. Move assets between Ethereum, Polygon, Arbitrum, Base, Optimism, BNB Chain, Solana, and more.

The bridge aggregates multiple underlying protocols to find the fastest, cheapest route for every transfer. It also supports fiat on-ramp — buy crypto with credit card and receive tokens directly on your destination chain.

Built by thirdweb, Universal Bridge is designed for both end-users and developers building DeFi applications that need seamless cross-chain payments.

Payment Methods

Multiple ways to fund cross-chain transfers — crypto wallets or traditional payment methods.

Crypto Wallet

Connect MetaMask, WalletConnect, Coinbase Wallet, or any Web3 wallet. Bridge tokens directly from your existing holdings across any supported chain.

Credit & Debit Card

Visa, Mastercard, and Apple Pay supported. Purchase tokens directly on your destination chain. No crypto exchange account required.

Bank Transfer

ACH, SEPA, and wire transfer supported for larger amounts. Lower fees than card payments with direct settlement to your wallet.

Stablecoin Payments

Use USDC, USDT, or DAI from any chain. The bridge handles cross-chain stablecoin transfers with minimal slippage and near-instant settlement.

Built for Developers

Integrate cross-chain payments into your app with the thirdweb SDK. React components, TypeScript, REST API.

// Bridge ETH from Ethereum to Base
import { Bridge } from "thirdweb/bridge";

const quote = await Bridge.getQuote({
  fromChain: "ethereum",
  toChain: "base",
  token: "ETH",
  amount: "1.0",
});

// Execute the bridge transaction
const tx = await Bridge.execute(quote);
console.log(`Bridged! TX: ${tx.hash}`);

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Universal Bridge?
Universal Bridge is a cross-chain payment and token bridging solution supporting 20+ blockchains including Ethereum, Polygon, Arbitrum, Base, Optimism, and Solana. It aggregates multiple bridge protocols to find the fastest, cheapest route and supports both crypto and fiat payments.
Which blockchains are supported?
Universal Bridge supports 20+ chains including Ethereum, Polygon, Arbitrum, Optimism, Base, BNB Smart Chain, Avalanche, Solana, zkSync, Linea, Scroll, Fantom, Gnosis, Celo, and more. New chains are added as the cross-chain ecosystem grows.
How does cross-chain bridging work?
The bridge locks your tokens on the source blockchain and mints or releases equivalent tokens on the destination chain. Universal Bridge aggregates multiple protocols (Across, Stargate, Hop, Connext) and selects the fastest, cheapest route for each transfer.
Can I pay with a credit card?
Yes. Universal Bridge supports fiat-to-crypto on-ramp via credit card, debit card, Apple Pay, and bank transfer. You can purchase tokens directly on your destination chain without first buying on an exchange and then bridging separately.
What are the fees?
Fees include a minimal platform fee plus underlying bridge protocol fees and network gas costs. The aggregator compares routes to minimise total cost. Typical bridge fees range from $0.50 to $5 depending on chain pair and congestion.
Is Universal Bridge safe?
Universal Bridge integrates only with audited bridge protocols. Smart contracts are verified by firms like OpenZeppelin. Transaction simulation and slippage protection are built in. However, cross-chain bridging carries inherent protocol risk — always verify details before confirming.
